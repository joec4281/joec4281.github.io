What single stock ETFs are available in Canada?

Assistant:

Single-stock ETFs in Canada are exchange-traded funds that provide exposure to the performance of an individual company's stock. Unlike traditional broad-market ETFs, these focus on one stock and typically incorporate strategies like covered calls (to generate higher yields through options premiums) or leverage (to amplify daily returns, often 2x or -2x). They are relatively new in the Canadian market, with launches accelerating in 2024-2025, and are listed on exchanges like the TSX or Cboe Canada. Major providers include Harvest Portfolios (with high-income covered call options), Purpose Investments (yield-focused covered calls), and LongPoint Asset Management (leveraged and inverse leveraged). Note that these products can be higher-risk due to options or leverage, and investors should review prospectuses for details like management fees, reset mechanics, and tax implications.

Below is a comprehensive list based on available offerings as of August 2025. This includes both Canadian and U.S. underlying stocks, as many are accessible to Canadian investors.
