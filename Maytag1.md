The Maytag MTW5605RW is a top-loading agitator washer with a 4.7 cubic foot capacity, designed for residential use and particularly suited for households with pets due to its specialized Pet Pro features. It offers robust cleaning performance with options like Extra Power for enhanced stain fighting and a built-in water faucet for pre-treating items.

### Key Features
- **Pet Pro System**: Includes a dedicated filter that removes up to 5x more pet hair compared to standard cycles, activated via the Pet Pro option; ideal for pet owners dealing with fur on clothes and linens.
- **Extra Power Option**: Boosts stain removal on any cycle by mixing hot and cold water for better cleaning without needing higher temperatures overall.
- **Power Impeller**: Provides vigorous wash action with triple spray to thoroughly soak and agitate clothes for effective soil and stain dissolution.
- **Auto Sensing**: Automatically adjusts water levels based on load size for efficient operation.
- **Built-In Water Faucet**: Allows for quick rinsing or soaking of items directly in the washer before starting a cycle.
- **Slow-Close Glass Lid**: Framed glass lid closes gently and provides a view of the wash action; the stainless steel drum is durable and rust-resistant.
- **Clean Washer with Affresh Cycle**: A maintenance cycle to keep the interior clean using Affresh tablets or bleach, recommended every 30 washes.

### Wash Cycles and Options
This model includes 11 wash cycles: Bulky Items, Casuals, Clean Washer with Affresh, Colors, Delicates, Drain & Spin, Heavy Duty, Normal, Quick Wash, Towels, Whites. It has 5 temperature settings (Cold, Cool, Hot, Tap Cold, Warm) and 3 wash/rinse temperatures. Additional options include 8 modifiers like Deep Fill (4 water levels: Auto, High, Max, Medium), Extra Rinse, Fabric Softener, Soil Level (3 levels), and combinations such as Pet Pro + Extra Power. No steam, sanitize, allergy, or delay start features.

### Warranty and Pricing
